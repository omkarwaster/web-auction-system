1. Introduction

Auction System is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you interact with our platform. By using our services, you agree to the practices described here. For any clarification, you can contact us via our Contact Page.

We respect your privacy and do not sell or trade personal information. Data may only be shared with:

5. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and industry best practices to protect your information. While no system can guarantee 100% protection, we regularly update our security measures.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you.
  • Update or correct inaccurate information in your account.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, enhance site performance, and deliver relevant content. You can manage cookie settings through your browser.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to operate the platform, meet legal requirements, or resolve disputes. Once no longer needed, data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications.
